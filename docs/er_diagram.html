Table Explanations

  • incoming_stock: Tracks all purchases (goods coming into the inventory), including supplier details, transaction amounts, and payment status.
  • outgoing_stock: Tracks all sales (goods going out to customers), including invoice details, final sale amounts, and payment status.
  • payment_transactions: Records partial or full instalment payments specifically linked to a purchase (incoming stock).
  • sales_payments: Records partial or full instalment payments specifically linked to a sale (outgoing stock).
  • product_details: Stores optional free-form details/notes about a specific product grouping by its name.
  • live_inventory: A virtual view calculated on-the-fly representing current stock (Sum of Incoming minus Sum of Outgoing).
